Hello, I’m Hayden.

I've worked in EMS my whole life.

Experience

I’ve been a long time Paramedic and EMT. Over the last 11 years I have worked the 911 systems spanning over 50 different cities and 3 states. Starting at the age of 18 I worked as an EMT in the greater Los Angeles area for numerous years. My career later took me to Massachusetts where I operated as a senior EMT mentoring the new hires in the greater Boston area. I later attended Paramedic School in Riverside CA, and after rigorous testing I recieved my license in 2016. I have been working as a Paramedic in the San Bernardino area ever since.

After thousands of patients treated over the years, it is now time to move on. I may lack web development experience, but my time as a paramedic has taught me how to learn at an accelerated rate as well as execute my tasks quickly and efficiently. Believe it or not, but emergency treatment is primarily comprised of customer service. Regardless of how stressful a situation may be, this experience allows me to stay professional, calm, and respectful at all times.
